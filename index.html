What is it

3 hours. Cross-functional teams. One real thing built.

A hackathon as team building. Cross-functional teams of 4 will build something small but real using Claude, AI tools, and anything else they need. Each team gets a dedicated spot in the office. At the end, every team does a 2-minute live demo. A jury reviews, and the winning team gets a prize.

Ideas & Challenges

Don't have an idea yet? No problem.

Below is a bank of challenge ideas to spark inspiration, but the best projects usually come from your own daily pain points. Think about what slows you down, what you wish existed, or what would make your team's life easier. You're free to pick one of these, remix them, or come up with something entirely new.

mail AI Inbox Triager

A bot that classifies incoming emails (candidates, clients, vendors) and drafts automatic responses. Useful for any team drowning in email.

smart_toy Internal Knowledge Bot

A chatbot trained on Nova's internal docs (processes, FAQs, policies) so anyone can find answers without digging through Drive.

leaderboard Lead Scorer

Analyze incoming leads and score them automatically based on fit with Nova's ideal client profile, prioritizing outreach for the Sales team.

podcasts Podcast Show Notes Generator

Given a podcast episode, auto-generate show notes, timestamps, key quotes, and ready-to-publish social media posts.

format_quote Testimonial Curator

Collect and organize member testimonials, categorize them by theme/outcome, and suggest the best ones for each context (web, pitch, social).

reviews Client Feedback Analyzer

Collect client feedback, identify patterns and sentiment, and generate actionable insights to improve service delivery.

school Onboarding Buddy

An AI agent that guides new Nova employees through their first weeks: explains processes, introduces tools, answers questions, and suggests who to talk to.

auto_awesome Event Recap Generator

After a Nova event, automatically generate a recap with highlights, key quotes from speakers, and a ready-to-share post for the community.

transform Content Repurposer

Turn any long-form content (blog post, podcast transcript, webinar) into multiple formats: newsletter snippet, LinkedIn post, tweet thread, Instagram carousel copy.

mood Nova Lux App

An upgraded, app-based version of Nova Lux: track team mood, surface trends, and generate weekly insights on how the team is doing.

mic AI Elevator Pitch Coach

A tool that listens to your pitch (for Nova, a client, a candidate) and gives real-time feedback on clarity, tone, and persuasion.

assistant Community Concierge Bot

An AI agent that helps members get the most out of the Nova community based on their profile, goals, and current moment: suggests events, connections, mentors, and resources.

These are just starting points. Feel free to come up with your own idea, combine several, or take one in a completely different direction.

FAQ

Got questions?

Do I need to prepare before the hackathon? add
Not required! But we encourage teams to meet beforehand to brainstorm ideas. Resources and tools will be available from Friday June 6th, so feel free to install and explore them to make the most of the 2h 30min build time.
Do I need to know how to code? add
Not at all. Teams are cross-functional on purpose. Builders, designers, strategists, domain experts: every skill matters. AI tools like Claude can help anyone contribute technically.
What can we build? add
Anything that solves a real problem at Nova or makes your team more efficient. Internal tools, automations, dashboards, prototypes: the format is open.
How do consulting sessions work? add
Each team gets up to 4 sessions of 10 minutes each. Just join the consultant's Google Meet link when you need help. Specialists cover Product, AI and Data topics, while generalists can help with broader tech questions.
What do we need to submit? add
Just a 2-minute live demo. No slides, no docs. Show what you built and why it matters.
How were the teams formed? add
Teams were designed to maximize diversity: each group mixes people from different departments and balances levels of experience with Claude and AI tools. The goal is that every team has the right ingredients to build something great.
